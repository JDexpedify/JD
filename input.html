FAQs

1. Who is this course for?

This course is designed for marketers, growth strategists, performance marketers, business owners, and AI enthusiasts who want to leverage AI for content creation, automation, and performance marketing execution.

2. Do I need prior experience in AI or coding?

No. While technical coding isn’t required, basic marketing knowledge is essential.

3. How is this course different from other marketing programs?

This is a 100% hands-on, AI-first, project-driven program with live training, exclusive tool access, and robust career support—unlike theory-based courses.

4. What tools will I learn?

You will gain hands-on experience with Expedify’s AI tool, ChatGPT, Jasper, Copy.ai, Zapier, Google AI, Meta AI, and other no-code AI automation tools.

5. What is the admission process?

Submit your application, take the qualifying marketing assessment, and if selected, you’ll receive an enrollment offer and pre-work materials.

6. Can I pay in installments?

Yes, flexible installment plans are available. Contact our admissions team for details.

7. Will I receive a certification?

Yes, upon successful completion you will receive an industry-recognized Expedify AI in Marketing Certification.

8. What if I need to defer my enrollment?

You can defer your enrollment to a future batch (within 6 months) at no extra cost or transfer your seat to an eligible candidate.
